What to do if you uploaded an incorrect PDF document?

Corrective Measure:

  • Enter the member's name and surname as (e.g., John Doe). If you do not do so in this required format this process will fail.
  • Enter the password given to you by the owner. If you do not know the password, ask the owner or a co-worker for this.
  • Once this is done, click "Remove Contract". The uploaded PDF will be removed, and you may then navigate to "Upload Contract" and upload the correct PDF.
