About AVS Fitness Gym

AVS is dedicated to providing top-notch fitness services and fostering a strong community of fitness enthusiasts. AVS Fitness Gym, founded in 2017 by Audrin Van Schoor, is more than just a gym—it's a beacon of resilience, dedication, and community. Nestled in the heart of Montana Park, Pretoria, AVS Fitness began as a modest venture, driven by Audrin’s lifelong passion for fitness and his desire to create a space where people could achieve their health and wellness goals. Early Beginnings (2017-2019) When Audrin Van Schoor opened the doors of AVS Fitness in 2017, his vision was clear: to provide top-notch fitness services that catered to both novices and seasoned athletes. The gym started small, focusing on personalized training sessions and a limited array of fitness classes. Word of mouth quickly spread, and AVS Fitness became known for its welcoming atmosphere, professional staff, and the genuine care Audrin showed for each member's fitness journey.


The Pandemic Pivot (2020) The year 2020 marked a turning point for AVS Fitness, as it did for many businesses worldwide. With the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, traditional ways of operating a gym were challenged. As lockdowns were enforced and public health became a priority, many fitness enthusiasts found themselves cut off from their regular exercise routines. Recognizing the critical importance of maintaining physical and mental health during these trying times, Audrin saw an opportunity to adapt and innovate. In response to the pandemic, AVS Fitness transitioned into a hybrid model, offering both in-person sessions with strict health protocols and a robust online platform. This pivot allowed the gym to not only survive the economic challenges brought by the pandemic but to thrive as a community hub where members could stay connected and motivated, even from their own homes. Audrin’s leadership during this period solidified AVS Fitness’s reputation as a forward-thinking and resilient fitness establishment.


Expansion and Growth (2024-Present) As the world began to adapt to the new normal, so too did AVS Fitness. The gym emerged from the pandemic stronger, with a broader vision and a more extensive community. In 2021, AVS Fitness expanded its facilities in Montana Park, adding state-of-the-art equipment, new fitness programs, and wellness services like nutritional counseling and mental health workshops. The focus was no longer just on physical fitness but on holistic well-being. Under Audrin's guidance, AVS Fitness became a cornerstone of the Montana Park community, hosting events, fitness challenges, and charity drives, all aimed at fostering a sense of belonging and mutual support. The gym’s commitment to its members and the broader community has made it a beloved institution in Pretoria.


Looking Ahead Today, AVS Fitness Gym is more than just a place to work out; it's a community where people come together to improve their lives, support one another, and celebrate their achievements. With a membership base that continues to grow, AVS Fitness remains dedicated to providing exceptional fitness services and fostering a strong, supportive community of fitness enthusiasts. As Audrin Van Schoor looks to the future, his vision for AVS Fitness continues to evolve. Plans are underway for further expansion, including additional locations and innovative fitness programs designed to meet the needs of an ever-changing world. Through it all, the core values of AVS Fitness—dedication, community, and resilience—remain unchanged, guiding every decision and ensuring that the gym will continue to thrive for years to come.
