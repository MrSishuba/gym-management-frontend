AVS Fitness Gym System Help Guide

Home

Welcome to AVS Fitness Home. This is the landing page for all Members. It provides general information focused on promoting community wellness, health, and promotional material that caters towards informing and helping members feel at home on the platform.

  • Click the "Read More" buttons to expand the paragraph text information.

Bookings

Navigate through this interface and find available lesson plans to choose from. Subscribed members have the ability to place bookings to attend classes at the Gym Studio.

  • How To book: Open the side navigation bar and click on the "bookings" once the screen loads you will find tabs for a specific lesson plan you can book for Click the book button and you will be presented with a calendar view where the availible slots will be displayed below, Select an availible date and click the time you wish to book for, confirm the booking and on completion you will be directed to "My Bookings page where you will be able to view, edit or cancel your booking otherwise your booking is made and you may attend the session at AVS Fitness gym"

AV Merch

This interface is primarily concerned with browsing AVS Fitness Merchandise and purchasing products.

  • What Can I Do? Click into the "AV Merch" tab and browse all available merchandise. Add items to your cart or wishlist and use the integrated paygate to complete your orders.
  • Receiving Orders: Once ordered successfully, make sure to check your profile. Go to orders to view orders and make sure to collect in person at the gym. There are no deliveries.
  • Late Collections: Make sure to collect within a 7-day period.

Profile

This interface is primarily concerned with managing yourself and your details.

  • Updating Personal Details: If any of your basic user details require a change, navigate to your profile, click the edit button, change your details, and save them.
  • Late Collections: Make sure to collect within a 7-day period.
  • Contract Management: Navigate to "Membership Management" and click the download button to get a copy of your signed contract. To receieve a copy of your consent form this is provided to members via email (AVSFitness@com) request with an emailed attachment of your consent form copy
  • Terminating Your Subscription: If you are unhappy or any issues necessitate you terminate your contract, navigate to membership management and follow the step-by-step process to complete this action.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I navigate the AVS Fitness system? Use the main menu to access different sections such as Home, Bookings, AV Merch, etc.
  • What if I encounter an issue? Contact system support or refer to this help guide for assistance.
  • How do I get in contact with an assistant? Call: 012 122 2456 Email: AVSFitness@com
  • I forgot my password: Navigate to the login screen and click "Forgot Password" fill in your current user email and click the reset button an email will be sent out to the provided email address where there will be a link to navigate you to the Change Password screen. There you will be able to create a new password and use the new password to login .
