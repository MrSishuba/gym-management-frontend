Our Policy and Code of Conduct

At AVS Fitness, we are committed to creating a safe and welcoming space for everyone. Our code of conduct outlines the behavior expected from our members and staff.

  • Respect for others and gym property
  • Proper gym attire at all times
  • Safe use of gym equipment
  • Prohibited behaviors: harassment, discrimination, and damage to property
  • Clean up after yourself and use provided sanitizing materials
  • Observe gym hours and closing times

Penalties for Violations

Violating the code of conduct may result in warnings, membership suspension, or permanent bans depending on the severity of the offense.
